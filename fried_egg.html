Fried Egg

A man, a good fried egg and i can work all morning so good!

Ingredients

  • One or more egg(s)
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Take a pan
  2. Put some cooking oil on the pan
  3. Starts to warm the pan on fire
  4. After the oil gets warm, put the egg on the pan and watch it
  5. Put some salt on the egg
  6. When the egg starts to frie, take a spoon and turn it
  7. Put some salt on the egg again
  8. While you do this, starts to press the egg so it will gets fried easily
  9. When you see that the egg is fried enough, cut the fire and take it to a plate and voilla! You got a nice fried egg.
