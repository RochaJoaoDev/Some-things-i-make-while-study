Popcorn

A good Popcorn isn't that hard to make

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Take a pan
  2. Put some cooking oil on the pan
  3. Starts to warm the pan on the fire
  4. After the oil gets warm, put the corn on the pan and cover it
  5. The corn will starts to pop, after it starts to stop of, you can cut the fire and voilla! You got some delicious Popcorn
